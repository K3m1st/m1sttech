Who I Am

I got into tech the same way I get into everything — by pulling at threads until something comes apart. That curiosity turned into security, security turned into building tools, and building tools turned into a Berkeley MICS degree and a data science certificate to make the foundations rigorous. I move fast, go deeper than required, and I'm honest about what I don't know. A homework assignment becomes a deployed system. A CTF becomes a methodology framework. I don't stop at the interesting part.

What I'm Building Toward

Offensive security is the core — red team, pentesting, vulnerability research. But I'm also building my own tools at the intersection of AI and security, and I'm open to wherever the most interesting work takes me. The goal is to ship things that matter, whether that's inside a company or on my own.
